## **The Hidden Bottleneck: Your Keyword Research Process**

You've probably heard the concept of semantic SEO. But here's the problem most marketers face: they skip the most critical first step **semantic keyword research**.

Without it, you're building on a weak foundation. You might create beautiful topic clusters, optimize entities perfectly, and implement internal linking flawlessly but if your initial keyword research is shallow, the entire strategy falls apart.
